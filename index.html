LUC BELAIRE FRANCE

Rosé

France’s Most Exquisite Rosé is a unique French sparkling Rosé with an extraordinary taste and beautiful, deep pink color. Belaire Rare Rosé is crafted by 5th and 6th generation winemakers at a Maison that traces its history to 1898. Our Rosé is available in seven iconic sizes.

Luxe

Indulge in True French Luxury. Produced in Burgundy, France from 100% Chardonnay grapes, Belaire Luxe also includes a special Chardonnay dosage as an integral part of the wine blend. Packaged in a luxurious white & gold bottle.

luxe rosé

Belaire Luxe Rosé epitomizes the qualities that have made the wines in our range so popular: classic taste, superb drinkability, and striking, stylish packaging. Luxe Rosé is an extraordinary wine from grape to glass, tailor-made to please crowds, anytime of year.

bleu

Belaire Bleu, our newest cuvée, is a perfect harmony of the old world and the new. Stunning aromas of fresh berries and tropical fruits are balanced with an elegant freshness and roundness to make this cuvée truly special. Its spectacular sapphire color, inspired by the beautiful blue waters of the Côte d’Azur, is ideal for dramatic cocktails as well!

Fantôme

The glowing Fantôme (French for “phantom”) editions of our signature Gold, Rosé, Luxe, and Luxe Rosé cuvées feature luminescent labels that light up at the press of a button. The Fantôme is ideal for celebratory moments after dark and is available by the bottle or by the glass in nightclubs, lounges and restaurants.
